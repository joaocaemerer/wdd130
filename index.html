The region boasts a blend of European influences, particularly from its significant population of Italian and German descendants. The capital, Porto Alegre, is a bustling city situated on the shores of the Gua√≠ba River, known for its lively atmosphere and cultural events. Rio Grande do Sul is also famous for its traditional gaucho culture, characterized by distinctive folk music, dance, and the practice of horsemanship.
